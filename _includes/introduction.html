Kang Liao 廖康

I am currently a Research Fellow at the MMLab@NTU, S-Lab, and working with Prof. Chen Change Loy, with the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. Before joining NTU, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2023 at Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU) in MePro, advised by Prof. Chunyu Lin. I was a visiting scholar at Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPII) in Computer Graphics Department advised by Vahid Babaei (Oct. 2021 - Oct. 2022). I have been fortunate to work with Yunchao Wei at BJTU, Xiangyu Xu at Sea AI Lab, Yulan Guo at National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), and Moncef Gabbouj at Tampere University.

I was born and raised in Anqing, a picturesque city with colorful culture in China.

Research Interests

My research interest lies at the intersection of Computer Vision and Deep Learning. In particular, my work focuses on image generation/restoration, multiple view geometry, 3D vision and point cloud, and 360° panoramic scene understanding, etc. I am also interested in reinforcement learning-based advanced manufacturing.
